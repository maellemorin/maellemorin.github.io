Gray Oncology Solutions

We were asked by the startup company Gray Oncology Solutions to design different parts of their product. We designed part of the user interface for their desktop app, we created a design guide and we redesigned their website. We worked on 3 features of the app (simulation, scheduling and care plans).
This is an ongoing project.

“GrayOS is the only platform on the market that fully automates patient scheduling while being built from the ground up for oncology.” -gray-os.com

WIREFRAMES

The Simulation is one of the core features of the app. It’s a dashboard where the user can create simulations of different scenarios for their clinic. Users would need to see the effect of different scenarios over the span of many months. After many iterations and brainstorming sessions, we decided a Gantt chart would be the best way to illustrate the simulation’s timeline.
