Alien Forest

The Alien Forest is a 3D environment built in Blender and assembled in Unity. User can explore this first-person game with the computer mouse and arrow keys. The story begins with a spaceship crashing on an alien planet. The spaceship is destroyed so the user needs to explore the planet to find a way out. A bridge and a large alien temple appear in the distance. A strange alien forest with unique trees occupies the ground under the bridge. While exploring the forest the user starts discovering its magic. The only way out is towards the sky, it is the player job to find it.

I was responsible for designing, modeling and texturing the temple, the flower tree and the ground. I also created the fog and waterfalls in Unity.
