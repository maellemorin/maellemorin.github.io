Space Explore

Space Explore is a 3D space simulation where users can explore gravitational fields and attraction forces. Users play the simulation like they were driving a spaceship. The space world consists of fictional planets orbiting around a sun. Not many instructions are given to the players : they need to explore the 3D world and discovered what they can customize on their own. A user interface will allow the player to change the physic values of the world to custom his/her own planets system. The speed orbit, the mass, the gravitational field, the distance between the sun and the planets are all values that can be tweaked by users.
