Quartz

Creation of a magazine concept done for an Editorial Design class at the Danish School of Media and Journalism.  Concept, identity, cover page and design were all done by myself. This publication features the artwork of the artist Nuria Madrid.

Quartz is a magazine for digital artists and for everyone who are digital art enthusiast. It is an indie magazine that is published twice a year. Its content targets young adults between 18-35 years old and features digital arts of every genre.
