A 3D environment built in Blender and assembled in Unity.

You crash lands on an alien planet. Your spaceship is destroyed so you explore the planet to find a way out. A bridge appears in front of you and you can see a large alien temple building in the distance. You walk towards it and cross over an alien forest. When you enter the temple, (triggering some action), gravity disappears and you float towards the sky.
