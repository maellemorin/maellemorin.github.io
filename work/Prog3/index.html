Rumplestiltskin Webpage

YEAR | Fall 2016
MEDIUM | html, css, javascript, jquery, photoshop
ROLE | Maëlle Morin: concept, design, coding
Eliane Robinson: concept, design, drawings

Project instruction: "Create a web page that is a playful/toy-like homepage for a fictional character from a novel/film/TV show/videogame/etc. of your choice."

The character we decided to choose was Rumpelstiltskin from the television show “Once Upon A Time”. The reason we chose him was because he was the character, we believed had to most to offer in terms of complexity of character. The tree represents the change of character in Rumple. Each icons represents a personality traits of Rumple and acts differently on the tree evolution.
