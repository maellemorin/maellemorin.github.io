Quartz Magazine

YEAR | 2018
MEDIUM | InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator

Magazine concept done for an Editorial Design class at the Danish School of Media and Journalism.

Quartz is a magazine for digital artists and for everyone who are digital art enthousiast. It is an indie magazine that is published twice a year. Its content targets young adults between 18-35 years old and features digital arts of every genre. . Quartz also has a website where new contents are published weekly. Its readers can find information on design studios, digital artists, tips to become a better artist and inspirations on www.quartz.com.
