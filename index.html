About Me

Maëlle Morin is a young digital artist who has a background in health sciences. She started doing signifiant artwork when she began her bachelor's degree in Computation arts in 2015. Internet, photography, graphic design and other digital technologies are the mediums she uses to express herself. Most of her work is experimental because she is still trying to find her own identity as an artist.
